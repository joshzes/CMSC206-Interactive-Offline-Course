CONGRATULATIONS for enrolling in CMSC 206 - Database Management Systems!

This Course will walk you through an exciting journey on how to manage your nutrition program in your local government units.

The NPM course in general is designed to teach local nutrition workers, members of the local nutrition committees, and other stakeholders on how to effectively plan, implement, monitor, evaluate, and sustain nutrition programs, in the context of the overall local governance and development system.

The Course consists of eight modules that cover various lessons to enhance your knowledge and skills in the area of local NPM and strengthen your core values as a nutrition worker. At the end of the course, you should be able to have a draft local nutrition action plan for your LGU.

For more details about this course, please study the Course Guide .
